About Us

Melinium Taekwando and Karate Academy, located in Ghana, stands as a beacon of martial arts excellence and inclusivity. Established in 2018, our academy has quickly earned its reputation as the most welcoming martial arts school in the country. Over the past five years, we have proudly welcomed over 200 international students, hailing from Europe and various African nations, into our fold.

At Melinium, we take great pride in our dedication to providing top-tier kung fu and karate training. Our students often commend the high standards of instruction, the comfortable learning environment, and the breathtaking backdrop of the surrounding mountains.

Our commitment to excellence extends beyond our academy's walls. We continuously strive to improve our martial arts programs, both in Ghana and internationally, with the aim of becoming the ultimate destination for those seeking to master the art of karate and martial arts.

Melinium Taekwando and Karate Academy has set its standards sky-high, ensuring our students leave with an unparalleled set of skills. We foster growth by organizing annual tournaments and competitions that allow our students to test their abilities in the fields of karate and taekwando. Additionally, we proudly sponsor students to participate in international tournaments across Europe and Asia, further enriching their martial arts journey. Join us at Melinium and experience the transformative power of martial arts while enjoying the support of a diverse and passionate community.
